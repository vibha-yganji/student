My Flappy Bird Game Improvement Ideas

  • More Obstacles: Add cool pipes for a real challenge.
  • Keep Score: Make a score system, so we know how awesome we are!
  • Game Over Screen: Show "Game Over" when we mess up – gotta make it fun.
  • Sound Effects: Bird sounds, pipe collisions, and happy music.
  • Background Music: Pick a fun tune that plays while we play.
  • Random Obstacles: Mix it up with pipes at different heights and spaces.
  • Visual Upgrades: Make bird and pipes look super cool with colors and images.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Let's play on our phones – touch controls for jumping!
  • High Score: Show off our best score as a badge of honor.
  • Instructions: Tell us how to play at the start – we're not mind readers!
  • Customize: Change birds and backgrounds to be unique.
  • Share Scores: Brag on social media about how awesome we are!
  • Testing: Get feedback from friends to make it even more fun.
  • Bug Hunt: Find and squash any annoying bugs that pop up.
  • Give Credit: If we use stuff from the internet, give props to creators.
  • Have Fun: Most important of all – make it a blast to play!
